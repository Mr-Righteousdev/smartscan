## üé§ Speaker Notes & Tips

### **Opening Strong (First 30 seconds)**
- Start with energy and enthusiasm
- Make eye contact with different audience members
- Use confident body language
- Speak clearly and at appropriate volume

### **During the Demo (5 minutes)**
- Face the audience, not the screen
- Explain each step before doing it
- Ask "Can everyone see this?" 
- Have backup screenshots ready
- Stay calm if technical issues occur

### **Engaging the Audience**
- Ask questions: "How many of you have heard of cybersecurity?"
- Use relatable examples: "Like protecting your mobile money"
- Encourage questions throughout
- Make it interactive, not just a lecture

### **Handling Questions**
- Repeat questions for the whole audience
- It's okay to say "I don't know, but I'll find out"
- Connect answers back to the simulation
- Use questions to demonstrate features
